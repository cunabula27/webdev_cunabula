I don’t think a work is ever really finished. The process of working and reworking ideas and themes is constant. With my newer video pieces especially, the outcome is an unrepeatable performance; what the audience sees and what is documented below happens live, in the moment.

However, it does occasionally come time to show work and I’ll collect it here. As of December 2013 when I started revising the site I’m going to add pieces when they were created so the top of this list in going to appear pretty static (though hopefully not for long).
