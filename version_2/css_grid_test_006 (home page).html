Hello

During 2013 I was kept very busy with temp0rary and I’ll be adding what we’ve been up to in the coming weeks. 2014 is already filling up with new projects, of which more soon.

In the meantime a good place to start would be the microsite for our last large-scale installation At Her Lady’s Pleasure (created with Kate Spence).

Output will collect together completed work, Process will be filling up with notes, sketches and works-in-progress and there’s a Morgue File, mainly for visual clippings from around the web and elsewhere. Finally, there’s information about past exhibitions, gigs and the development of the site in the About section.

You can also find me on Flickr, Vimeo and Twitter or if you’d like to get in touch.

Work will also soon be appearing at cunabulapress.com more focused on book art, print and design projects. I expect there’ll be a bit of crossover between the two sites but we’ll never know unless I get all the documentation together.

Right, back to the pump.
